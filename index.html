The Science of Sound

By Rio Miyakawa Baptista

Sound is everywhere, wherever we go. We use sound as a form of communication and entertainment as well as an aid to a balance and direction. We can also use sound sound for other tasks such as mapping, medical uses, and pest control. I chose this project because i wanted to explore more about how you can interact with sound and the fundamentals of this "sound" that we use everyday.
